# Introduction to Algorithmic Thinking

Algorithmic thinking is the process of breaking down a problem into a series of steps that can be followed to solve it. It's a fundamental skill in computer science, but it's also a valuable skill in many other fields.

In this section, you'll learn the basics of algorithmic thinking, including:

*   What is an algorithm?
*   How to design an algorithm.
*   How to analyze an algorithm's efficiency.
*   Common algorithmic patterns.
